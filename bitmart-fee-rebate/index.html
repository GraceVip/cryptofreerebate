What to confirm

BitMart may display a higher rebate headline than other exchanges, but terms can change. Confirm the benefit on the registration page and read all product and account limitations.

  • Check the invite code or referral campaign shown during registration.
  • Confirm whether the headline applies to all trading products or only selected products.
  • Review eligibility, regional limits, and account requirements directly with the exchange.
  • Use the calculator to estimate whether the rebate matters for your expected volume.

Responsible framing

A rebate can reduce trading costs, but it does not reduce market risk. Do not trade only to earn rebates.
