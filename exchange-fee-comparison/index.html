Exchange fee comparison

Crypto exchange fee comparison checklist.

Exchange fee comparison should include more than the public maker and taker fee table. Check base fees, fee rebate links, referral discounts, trading products, withdrawal costs, and regional eligibility.

What to compare

Fee factor Why it matters Where to check
Maker and taker fees They affect every trade Exchange fee schedule

Fee comparison pages

Start with the exchange-specific pages for Binance, OKX, Bitget, and BitMart. Then estimate the result with the crypto fee rebate calculator.

Do not compare rebates in isolation

A fee rebate is part of the total cost, not the whole decision. Liquidity, spreads, available order types, security controls, withdrawal support, tax records, and platform availability can matter more than a small fee difference.
