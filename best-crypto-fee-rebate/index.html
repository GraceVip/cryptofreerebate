How to choose a rebate link

Start with the exchange you would use even without a rebate. A fee rebate only helps if the platform supports your country, assets, order types, deposits, withdrawals, and risk preferences.

Then compare the total fee path. A smaller rebate on a lower base fee can sometimes beat a bigger rebate on a higher base fee. Use the crypto fee rebate calculator to estimate monthly and annual savings from your own trading volume.

What to verify on the registration page

  • The referral code or invite link is visible before account creation.
  • The rebate percentage or discount wording matches what you expected.
  • The benefit applies to the products you plan to use, such as spot or futures.
  • Your region, KYC status, and account history are eligible.
  • The exchange fee table still matches your expected base rate.
