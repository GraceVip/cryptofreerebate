FAQ

Crypto fee rebate FAQ.

What is a crypto fee rebate?

A crypto fee rebate is a trading fee benefit connected to an exchange referral, invite link, campaign, or account tier. The exact benefit depends on the exchange, region, product, and current campaign terms.

How do I compare crypto fee rebate links?

Compare the visible referral code, rebate percentage, supported products, region rules, KYC requirements, and whether the benefit applies to spot, futures, or both.

Are crypto fee rebate offers guaranteed?

No. Crypto fee rebate offers depend on platform terms, region, KYC status, deposits, trading activity, and campaign timing.

Is this investment advice?

No. Crypto Free Rebate only provides mainstream trading platform registration links, fee discount information, and calculators. It does not recommend buying, selling, holding, or trading any asset.

Can a CEX also offer stock trading?

Yes, but the structure matters. Some platforms offer real stock trading. Others offer tokenized stocks that provide price exposure but may not include shareholder rights. Always verify product access on the platform site.

Do all platforms support every region?

No. Each crypto platform can restrict registration, products, fee rebates, KYC rules, deposits, and stock or tokenized stock access by country or region. Always check the platform page for your location before registering.
