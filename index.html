FAQ

Crypto fee rebate FAQ.

What is a crypto fee rebate?

A crypto fee rebate is a trading fee benefit connected to an exchange referral, invite link, campaign, or account tier. The exact benefit depends on the exchange, region, product, and current campaign terms.

How do I check CEX fee discount links?

Check the visible referral code, fee discount wording, supported products, region rules, KYC requirements, and whether the exchange page confirms the benefit.

Are crypto fee rebate offers guaranteed?

No. Crypto fee rebate offers depend on platform terms, region, KYC status, deposits, trading activity, and campaign timing.

Is this investment advice?

No. Crypto Free Rebate only provides selected centralized crypto exchange registration links, fee discount information, and calculators. It does not recommend buying, selling, holding, or trading any asset.

Do all platforms support every region?

No. Each centralized exchange can restrict registration, crypto products, on-chain asset access, U.S. stock access, fee rebates, KYC rules, deposits, and account access by country or region. Always check the platform page for your location before registering.

Need a registration check?

Ask before opening an account.
